# Hitchmap Privacy Policy

*We aim to keep this privacy policy as clear, concise, and easy to understand as possible. We are committed to being transparent and open. This policy explains how we collect information about you, and what we do with that information once we have it.*

---

## 1. Data Controller

Hitchmap operates this application. For privacy-related questions, contact us at [info@hitchmap.com](mailto:info@hitchmap.com).

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us or that is generated through your use of the app:

- **Account & profile information:** name, email address, profile picture, gender, age, and general location (city/region)
- **Social identifiers:** usernames from linked third-party networks
- **App usage data:** hitchhiking experiences and map contributions you submit
- **Language preferences**

We do not collect precise GPS location data without your explicit consent.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Operate and maintain the Hitchmap service
- Display your public profile to other members
- Conduct anonymised research on hitchhiking and mobility patterns (see our [statistics page](https://hitchmap.com/dashboard.html))
- Respond to your support requests

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process your data on the following legal bases:

- **Contractual necessity** – to provide the Hitchmap service
- **Legitimate interests** – to improve the service and conduct mobility research
- **Consent** – where you have explicitly agreed (e.g. optional profile fields)

---

## 5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

- We **never sell** your personal data to any third party
- We do not use your data for commercial advertising purposes
- Aggregated, anonymised data may be used for research and published in our public statistics

---

## 6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is securely hosted on servers located within the **European Union**, in compliance with GDPR requirements.

---

## 7. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

- **Access:** view your profile information at any time within the app
- **Edit:** update or remove personal information from your profile at any time
- **Delete:** request full account and data deletion by contacting [info@hitchmap.com](mailto:info@hitchmap.com)
- **Portability:** request a copy of your personal data
- **Objection:** object to processing based on legitimate interests

GDPR users may also lodge a complaint with their local data protection authority.

---

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. Upon a deletion request, your data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

Hitchmap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---
